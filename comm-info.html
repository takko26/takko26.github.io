Rules

Payment

  • Payment via Paypal Invoices only, must be paid up-front, I won't start work until paid.

  • All Prices are in USD (United States Dollars), please convert them accordingly to your country's currency when calculating prices.

  • Furthermore, don't send money before I ask for payment or accept the commission.

  • No refunds once I start work on the commission or after it's done.

Content

  • No NSFW, gore, content insulting for LGBT+ people, hateful or politics-related content.

  • Please don't ask for uncomfortable fetish art, or suggestive art of underage characters.

  • I reserve my right to deny your commission request if I'm uncomfortable with it.

  • Ask permission for commissioning OCs that don't belong to you, my characters are free to being commissioned as long as it doesn't break the previous rules.

Guidelines

  • Keep your request under 1000 letters, I work quicker with simple requests.

  • Commissions will be begin to be accepted after all requests have been read, small priority on earlier ones and depending on the spots open.

  • Use prices for reference before buying, I'll give you an estimate if necessary.

  • Please provide colored visual references with your request description.
    Preferably with an imgur(or similar) link.
    I won't draw a character from scratch or from description alone.

  • Specify if you want me to replicate a style I've done before.
    I won't replicate other local artist's styles.

  • I usually post the finished pieces on my social media.
    Ask me if you want your artwork to be private or anonymous.

  • Once paid I will send a rough sketch for revision, if you ask for significant changes after this they'll cost extra.

  • When your commission is finished I'll provide the archive in full resolution via Sta.sh, imgur, or the image hosting service you'd prefer.

Commission Details

Character Artwork

    Full-body

  • Character art with high detail made to your preferences
  • Max resolution 3000 x 3000
  • Can be expanded with multiple characters on shot, background, props, etc. for extra price.

    Bust/Icon

  • Simple composition drawing perfect for use as Avatar/Profile picture or Emote on Twitch, Dircord, etc.
  • Max resolution 2000 x 2000
  • Single character per illustration

    Extra characters

  • An extra character will be an additional 50% of the commission's base price.
  • Adding two extra character will be an additional 90%.
  • I generally try to avoid doing over 2 extra characters per commission.

    Background

  • I always send the transparent version but I'll try to add a very simple color background to compliment the image for no cost.
  • Simple Location refers to backgrounds that aren't defined but work as a small compliment to the scene, like a beach or a generic bedroom.
  • Complex or from description backgrounds are if you want a scene taking place in a specific area you described in your request.

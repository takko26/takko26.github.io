Extras

  • Extra characters:
    60% of the standard cost per extra character (May depend in complexity).

    Here's an example, if you want to buy a two character commission, both full-body and full-shaded, the price will be $[45] + $[45x60%] = $[45] + $[27] = $[72]

  • Backgrounds:

    Generally I'll add a very simple background to accompany the image for no cost, but these aren't controlled by the commissioner, if you want a specific background done it will cost extra.

    Background prices will vary on amount of detail, and accounting for extra characters and props designed, here's some guides for how I classify them.

    -Simple BG: Colors and patterns like dots or checkers, with a 2-4 color palette.
    -Standard BG: Scenery like a beach or a forest behind the characters, nothing happening in the back, only for complimenting the image.
    -Complex BG: If you want a scene taking place in a specific area with the characters interacting with the background.

  • Extra Details:
    (Only for shaded and detailed option)
    If you're looking for me to replicate a style I've done before please ask, and provide references if possible, I usually won't ask for extra unless you're asking after commission has already been started, so don't forget those details.
